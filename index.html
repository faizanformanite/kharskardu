Crafted from Tradition, Built for Comfort

The Khar is more than just a place to stay; it's an experience woven from the threads of local heritage. Our guesthouse is constructed with locally sourced wood, handcrafted by artisans who have passed down their skills through generations.

We invite you to disconnect from the everyday and reconnect with nature in an environment that prizes authenticity, comfort, and quiet luxury.
